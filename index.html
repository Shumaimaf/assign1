The Management team of this company has a diversified experience of a multifaceted segment of tourism, travel and hospitality industry of many years in Canada and Pakistan. Karachi

Karachi is the capital of the Pakistani province Sindh. It is the most populous city in Pakistan, and sixth-most-populous city proper in the world. Ranked as a beta world city

Islamabad

Islamabad, the capital city of Pakistan and it is federally administered as part of the Islamabad Capital Territory.Islamabad is noted for its high standards of living, safety, and abundant greenery

Lahore

Lahore is a city in the Pakistan,in the province of Punjab. Lahore is the country’s second-most populous city after Karachi and is wealthiest cities with an estimated GDP of $58.14 billion of 2015
